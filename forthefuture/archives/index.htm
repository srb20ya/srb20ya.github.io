Timeline

June 2012 - Tobi discovers SRB2 on Softonic.

January 15th, 2017 - Tobi uploads 2 videos, being private, which is footage from ZDoom, playing Sonic Doom 2 v4.1.

May 2017 - The site is created, however, since I did not know HTML at the time, I made it in a text file.

June 25th, 2017 - Tobi uploads 2 videos of Sonic Robo Blast 1.3f.

October 31st, 2017 - Tobi uploads the original SRB2 Halloween 1.0 video.

November 1st, 2017 - Tobi uploads the original SRB2 Halloween 1.32 video.

November 2017 - Tobi obtains a Compaq Notebook Evo N150 from 2000, running Windows 98.

March 2018 - SRB2 2.2 Anniversary Trailer is released. Tobi becomes inspired.

May 3rd, 2019 - Tobi uploads the Sonic Robo Blast 1.3f soundtrack.

June 16th, 2019 - Tobi uploads the first footage of Xmas 0.92-0.94 in nearly 20 years.

August 1st, 2019 - Tobi uploads the soundtrack to Jeff Read's Sonic Doom, but with the Roblox death sound as a soundfont.

December 31st, 2019 - Earliest page archive.

March 8th, 2020 - SRB20YA finds a host at 000webhost.

March 23rd, 2020 - Discord server created.

May 8th, 2020 - After issues with file types, Tobi switches the host to a friend's server.

May 15th, 2020 - Tobi switches again to NeoCities.
