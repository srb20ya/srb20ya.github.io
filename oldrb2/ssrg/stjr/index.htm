Welcome to Sonic Team Junior's website,
where we're dedicated to bringing you top-quality, fan-made, "Sonic the Hedgehog"-based games - for free!

- Site sections -

Descriptions of the individual sections on the STJr website

STJr Information
About STJr | Members
Information on who we are, and what we do, plus our history of producing games.

News and Updates
For the latest on current events regarding STJr, its members, and the website.

Games
SRB 1 | SRB 1.5 | SRB 2 | Sonic: EQ-RPG
Information, news, reviews and screenshots from our "Sonic Robo Blast" series, as well as our forthcoming game, "Sonic: Emerald Quest RPG".

Downloads
Download early previews and full versions alike of our games, as well as add-ons and multimedia clips.

Previews
Previews of updates to our existing releases, as well as forthcoming titles.

STJr Message Board
The forum where you can talk to members of the STJr team, or voice your opinions on subjects regarding STJr, or even just have a good time chatting with other fellow Sonic fans!

Polls
UPDATED The voting section of the site, where you can help us decide on what we should add to our games, or whatever happens to be the subject of the poll. Perhaps even just for fun!

Competitions
Your chance to win prizes by completing challenges based in STJr games.

The White Glove Diaries
What started out as a diary of the construction of SRB 2, has now spawned into an editorial section of the site, with articles from Sonikku and DJ Mike.

Help
Hints and Tips | Software Bugs | Technical Support
If you have a problem with one of our games, think you've found a new bug, or just need some help to get you past that too-difficult-part-of-a-level, then you need only look here!

Links
Links to the websites of STJr associates, popular Sonic sites, and other favourites of the STJr team.

That's all from us - Enjoy your visit!
