A list of links to other Sonic / Sega based websites.

The Sonic Zone - The Sonic Zone was the first ever site to host our first game, Sonic Robo Blast. This site has got over 125,000 hits and has been going for around two years, with the second best Sonic-based news on the Net. Definitely worth a visit!

The Sonic Fan Game HQ - As you can probably work out from the name, this site is devoted entirely to Sonic Fan Games, with reviews, previews, sprites for your own games and much, much more.

Electro Sonic Team - Mostly devoted to fan-made games, but has some other Sonic-related stuff here too. Dan is now concentrating on making his own games.

The Sonic Foundation (tSf) - It's tricky to say which site has more hits... Sometimes it's tSf and sometimes it's the Sonic HQ - Either way, any site with over 250,000 hits has to be worth a visit! It has far too much stuff to mention here.

Shadow of Light – Another  large Sonic site – taking it’s cue from Total Neon and Perfect Chaos with a Photo Album, Chao stuff, VMU files, movies, games, codes, previews, wallpapers, and the brilliant Casinopolis. A great site.


The Sonic HQ - Another one of the original sites, and features almost everything you could possibly want or need with Sonic-related stuff.

Segasonic.net - Probably the best site on the net for music and Sonic Anime! STJr used to be served here, but due to continual server problems, decided to move, though these are being ironed out now.

SSRG: The Sonic Stuff Research Group - If you're interested in things like Sonic 2 Beta and trying to find out more about the hidden levels in Sonic 2, then this is definitely the place to go. STJr is now being served here!

EmulaionZone - Alright, it's not strictly Sonic related, but how are you going to play Sonic 2 Beta without a Genesis emulator?

Windy Valley Cards - Here's something no-one else has done before... Sonic Team based cards!

The Sonic Ideal - One of the biggest Sonic fan-made comic on the 'Net, created by fellow STJr member, Dave Bulmer!

The Hidden Palace Zone - A site devoted to perhaps the more hard-core Sonic fans, but still a good read, none the less, with some fair comments on Sonic Robo Blast! Features all the downloadable SA goodies from the Sonic 2K events to the holiday specials.

The Super Sonic Zone - It seems any site with 'Sonic Zone' has good news coverage. TSSZ is no exception, providing outstanding news coverage on everything, from Dreamcast news to fan-games and fan-comics. If you're wondering what’s new in the Sonic world, check this site out... it beats even the great Sonic Zone!

STC: STEM – A site truly devoted to the UK Sonic comic, Sonic the Comic, check this site out for exclusive previews and reviews, as well as a monthly editorial magazine.

Fan Game Team links.

By no means are we the only people who make fan-games. There are dozens of fan game teams out there. Here's just some of them:

Project Blue streak Dev. Group - They've got some nice fan games coming along in C, whereas most people are just using Click n' Create or the Games Factory. It'll be a good site when it's done... except for perhaps the oh-too-familiar site design... ;-D

Sonikku Team - Recently, have just come into the fan games scene... with a bang. Metal Sonic Quest is considered the new SRB1-beater by some people... excellent stuff.

Sonic: Project Mettrix - Another fan-made game made by STJr's very own Ron! This game is shaping up to be the best 2D fan-game ever!

 

Focus Games – Edging more between making Quake levels and Pokemon games, but there’s enough here to keep fan-gamers happy!
