11th March 2000 -

A New Hope.

Stealth has left the building, again.

There has been a lot of misunderstandings and misconceptions about this whole matter, and to be brief, both of us are a little at fault.

Basically, Stealth was pretty polite and left STJr without any grudges, but I then later found out that he'd apparentely been insulting one of our programmers while he left, something I didn't think was quite so polite and jolly.

Which led to this impression that Stealth was just after the money we gave him, while he thought we were trying to get ourselves 'infinite fame and glory' from this one technique the programmer he had apparentely slagged off (he didn't.) to make the engine run faster, but I was unaware of the consequences, and things got into a tumbled mess from there.

So a misunderstanding on both parts - things have been resolved now, thankfully without any major battles or flamewars, however, though we've lost a major programmer, things aren't all bad. A.J is getting even better at programming, and has managed to fix multiplayer mode, and is adding plenty of new features for SRB2:XMAS v0.93.
