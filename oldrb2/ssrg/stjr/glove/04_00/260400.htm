26th April 2000 -

The contest!

 

After weeks of nail biting, the contest is drawing to a close. By early May, we’ll have the winners, ready to claim their prizes. At least 10 people will walk away with the Millennium level and 5 will have greater prizes.

 

JoshKnux seems to (again) be winning with the 28 second time, but Rlan and HEDGESMFG are in hot pursuit for the soundtrack… the times are insanely close, with tenths of a second separating winners. It seems winners of this contest will be in 28-32 second region.

 

Mind you, Ada-Ring has been working on his time, and got 26 seconds, but alas, suffered from a corrupt demo due to an incorrect version, but he still has time to work on his demo and who knows? He could draw level or defeat Josh!
