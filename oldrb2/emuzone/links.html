Check out other Sonic and fan-game sites around the web!

General Sonic Links


These are general Sonic sites, with pretty much everything you need from music, art files, information about new and upcoming Sonic releases, and so on.

The Official SRB2 site - The 'brother' to this site is dedicated solely to SRB2. Go here for the very latest news pertaining to SRB2.

Sonic Team - The creators of the original games, this can't go without a mention! Check out the Team's newest games, and of course - Sonic Adventure 2!

Sonic HQ - This is a pretty impressive Sonic site with pretty much everything about Sonic. With over half a million hits, this site is well worth a visit.

The Sonic Foundation - This is another big Sonic site that can be found on the web, featuring a pretty impressive collection of fan-art and fan-fics.

SSNTails.Org - After SegaSonic.Net went down, SSNTails.Org began in it's wake. You can obtain the files off SegaSonic.Net from here (such as the Sonic Anime, and the Sonic sprite sheets).

The Sonic Stuff Research Group - If you're interested in things like Sonic 2 Beta and trying to find out more about the hidden levels in Sonic 2, then this is definitely the place to go. This site has been the winner of The Sonic Emmy Award's 'Best Sonic site' category, so it must be pretty special!

The Sonic Ideal - Probably THE biggest Sonic fan comic on the Internet right now. Based entirely in the SegaSonic universe, it could answer several fans' prayers about a good SegaSonic-based comic.

The Hidden Palace Zone - This site focuses more on finding out how the innards of Sonic Adventure work, along with a good downloads section where you can get new files for SA if you ever get bored with the ones Sega provide you.

The Super Sonic Zone - If you want the very latest news on Sonic, Sega and fan-projects, this is the best place to go. The news here is not only regularly updated, but has the news available in several different formats - such as the radio shows you can hear. Impressive stuff.

Team Artail - The biggest Sonic fan-art site on the web! Featuring thousands of pictures from an archive of a great many fan-artists. Also home to the SatAM petition.


Fan Game Links

These are sites mostly devoted to fan-games, we are by no means the only fan-game team out there - there must be hundreds of teams out there, so are some links to some of the fan-game sites and teams...

The Sonic Fan Made Games HQ - As you can tell by the name, this is the biggest site on the Internet for news, interviews, downloads and add-ons for your favourite Sonic fan games. Definately worth a visit.

SAGE - SAGE (Sonic Amateur Games Expo) is the equivilant of E3, and offers a large variety of the best fan-games - the next expo is due to happen on February 9th 2001. Turn up here: It'll be well worth it.

MagicgrafX - Creators of the famous 'Sonic Chaos' series, currently featuring the best Sonic engine made under Click n' Create.

Sonic Epoch - Sonic Epoch is the first SatAm based fan-game, features RPG elements and is also fully programmed. So far the game lasts about 2-3 hours, and that's just for a 20% complete demo. Pretty impressive stuff.

Gregorsoft - Creators of 'Sonic Ultra', another popular fan-game series made under Click n' Create.


Other Sites

These sites don't pertain much to Sonic or the fan-gaming community, but instead are either generally about videogames or are just very, very funny.

RPGamer - Looking for information on the best RPGs? Check this site out, offering news, previews, reviews, editorials, music and sound bytes from your favourite RPGs!

The Encyclopedia of Pointlessness - How many pizzas will fit into a piece of cheese? The answer, along with other questions on your mind (such as 'Life, The Universe and Everything') can be found here. Oh: And other stuff, too.

Sluggy Freelance - Perhaps one of the best Internet comics out there. Very wacky and enjoyable; well worth your time.
