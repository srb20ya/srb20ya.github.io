Previews / Features.

We have released a 60% complete alpha of SRB2! Sonic Robo Blast II Christmas Test is available for you to download below!

What will SRB2 feature? It will be the first completed Sonic the hedgehog based fan-created game in 3D, have support for Network, Serial, Modem and Internet play and it's own soundtrack. Play alone to collect all the seven chaos emeralds and thwart the evil Dr. Eggman's plans to rule the world with the Eggrock or play together to stop him... or play against each other!

Welcome to the next dimension in Sonic fan gaming.
