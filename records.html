Timed world records.


RULES

For runs before Halloween 1.0, you must video record your run. Halloween and above have demo recording, inherited from DOOM. You may use either.
No splicing.
No cheats.
Runs start as soon as you gain character control.
Runs end at:
SRB1: When you finish all the Hyper levels
TGF and C: When the last stage is cleared
SRB1 Christmas: When the AoStH scene finishes
Halloween: When you finish the last stage
Christmas: When the final boss is defeated
Demo 1: When you reach the end of GFZ1
Demo: When the last stage is cleared
Except for the 2D Era and SRB1 categories, time is tracked by the time score at the end of the stage.
Submissions are to be made here.
